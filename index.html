Fun Facts

  • Live in groups called prides
  • Average pride size is 15 lions
  • Territory can range from 20 square km to 400 square km
  • Hunt everything from rodents to hippos, and even elephants
  • In the savana prides, females to most of the hunting. But in woodland areas, the males hunt frequently.
